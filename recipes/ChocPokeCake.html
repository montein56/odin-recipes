CHOCOLATE POKE CAKE

Description

pending

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 5 squares Bakers unsweetened chocolate
  • 1 cup luke warm strong black coffee
  • ¾ cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 1 pkg chopped white chocolate
  • ¼ cup whipping cream
  • ¼ cup butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, unsweetened chocolate, black coffee, sour cream and oil.
  2. Beat on high for 3 minutes or till blended.
  3. Pour into greased and floured 9” spring-form pan.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour or till done.
  5. Cool for 10 minutes
  6. Poke with wooden spoon
  7. Melt chopped white chocolate with butter and whipping cream over a low heat.
  8. Stir till smooth
  9. Cool lightly and pour over cake.
  10. Garnish using chocolate curls or grated chocolate.
