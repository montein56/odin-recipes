Chocolate Truffles

Description

One basic recipe with a variety of liqueurs and coatings to choose from.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 pks (175g) semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla

    • Coatings:

  • chopped nuts, sprinkles, coconut, etc

STEPS

  1. In a heavy saucepan over low heat melt chips with condensed milk.
  2. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla.
  3. Chill 2 hours or until firm.
  4. Shape into 1 inch balls, roll in coatings.
  5. Chill 1 hour or until firm.
  6. Store covered at room temperature. Makes about 6 dozen.
